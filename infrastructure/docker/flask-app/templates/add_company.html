Tips
Required Fields:
  • Ticker Symbol - Must be unique
  • Company Name
Finding RSS Feeds:
  1. Visit the company's IR page
  2. Look for "RSS", "Subscribe", or feed icon
  3. Right-click and copy the feed URL
  4. Test the URL in your browser
Finding Newswire ID:
  1. Visit the company's IR page
  2. Click on any recent press release
  3. Look at the URL for the organization ID
  4. Example URL: /3231625/34254/en/
  5. The ID is 34254
