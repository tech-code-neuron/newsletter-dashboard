Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

The REIT Sheet ("we", "us", "our") provides a daily email digest of REIT press releases. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you subscribe to our newsletter.

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to The REIT Sheet, we collect:

  • Email address - Required to send you the newsletter
  • IP address - Recorded at signup for fraud prevention
  • Signup timestamp - When you subscribed
  • Engagement data - Email opens and link clicks (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send you our daily REIT press release digest
  • Process unsubscribe requests
  • Improve our newsletter content and delivery
  • Prevent abuse and fraud

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Tracking

Our emails may contain tracking pixels and tracked links to measure engagement. This data is used in aggregate to improve content quality. You can disable image loading in your email client to prevent open tracking.

Data Retention

We retain your email address and subscription data while you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we keep a record of your email in our suppression list to honor your preference. Engagement event data is automatically deleted after 2 years.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe - Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link
  • Data export - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Data deletion - Request permanent deletion of your data
  • Correction - Update your email address

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@reitsheet.co.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (AWS)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security reviews

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) - Email delivery via SES, data storage

These providers have their own privacy policies governing data handling.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to active subscribers.
